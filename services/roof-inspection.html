Comprehensive Roof Inspections

A professional roof inspection is the first step in protecting your property investment. At Preferred Roofing & Exteriors, we offer thorough, free roof inspections throughout Atlanta and surrounding areas. Our certified inspectors examine every aspect of your roof to identify current issues, potential problems, and provide recommendations for maintenance or repairs.

Whether you're buying a home, filing an insurance claim, or simply want to know your roof's condition, our detailed inspection reports give you the information you need to make informed decisions.

What We Inspect

  • Shingle condition and integrity
  • Flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights
  • Gutters and drainage systems
  • Roof structure and decking
  • Signs of leaks or water damage
  • Ventilation and insulation
  • Storm damage assessment

When to Get a Roof Inspection

1

After Severe Weather

Following storms, hail, or high winds to assess damage for insurance claims.

2

Before Buying or Selling

Ensure you know the roof's condition before making a major investment decision.

3

Annual Maintenance

Regular inspections help catch problems early before they become costly repairs.

4

Suspected Issues

If you notice leaks, missing shingles, or other signs of roof problems.
