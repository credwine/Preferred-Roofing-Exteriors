Complete Gutter Solutions

Properly functioning gutters are essential for protecting your home from water damage. At Preferred Roofing & Exteriors, we provide comprehensive gutter services including installation, repair, replacement, and cleaning throughout Atlanta and surrounding areas.

Our experienced team works with quality materials and proven installation techniques to ensure your gutters effectively channel water away from your home, protecting your foundation, siding, and landscaping.

Our Gutter Services

Gutter Installation

Professional installation of seamless and sectional gutters in various materials and styles to match your home.

  • Seamless gutters
  • Aluminum & copper options
  • Custom sizing and colors

Gutter Repair

Fast repairs for sagging, leaking, or damaged gutters to restore proper function and prevent water damage.

  • Leak repairs
  • Sagging fixes
  • Downspout repairs

Gutter Cleaning

Regular cleaning to remove debris, leaves, and blockages that can cause overflow and water damage.

  • Debris removal
  • Downspout flushing
  • Maintenance plans

Gutter Guards

Installation of gutter guard systems to reduce maintenance and prevent clogs from leaves and debris.

  • Screen systems
  • Mesh guards
  • Reduced maintenance

Why Proper Gutters Matter

  • Protect your foundation from water damage and erosion
  • Prevent basement flooding and water intrusion
  • Protect siding and exterior walls from water damage
  • Prevent landscape erosion and soil displacement
  • Maintain your home's value and curb appeal
